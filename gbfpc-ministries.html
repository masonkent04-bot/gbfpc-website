GBFPC's ministries are more than programs — they're families. Whether you're a first-time visitor or a lifelong member, each ministry exists to help you grow in your faith, build genuine relationships, and make a real difference in Bakersfield and beyond.

Each ministry is led by passionate, Spirit-filled leaders who are committed to walking alongside you every step of the way.
