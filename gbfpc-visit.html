Your First Visit

Here's What
to Expect.

GBFPC is a warm, welcoming family that takes faith seriously without taking itself too seriously. Come comfortable, come curious, and come ready to encounter God in a genuine way.

Our services blend powerful worship with practical, Scripture-based teaching — and you'll always find friendly faces ready to help you feel at home.

01
Arrive & Park

Ample parking is available on-site. Our team will greet you at the door and point you in the right direction.

02
Drop Off Kids

K.I.C.K. children's ministry is available during Sunday services for ages 4–12. Safe, fun, and Spirit-filled.

03
Worship Together

Expect powerful praise and worship led by our team, followed by a message grounded in Scripture and relevant to your life.

04
Stay & Connect

After service, our Connect team will welcome you, answer questions, and help you take a next step — no pressure.
