Our Mission

Raising the
Next
Generation.

Bethel Apostolic Academy (BAA) and Kiddie Korral (BKK) are the educational and childcare ministries of GBFPC — a place where academic excellence and Christian values come together to form the whole child. We believe that nurturing a child's mind, heart, and spirit is one of the highest callings we can answer.

Whether your child is just starting life at Kiddie Korral or finishing their senior year at Bethel Academy, every child who walks through our doors receives the attention, love, and instruction they need to thrive — in the classroom and in life.

Academic Excellence

Rigorous, age-appropriate curriculum that builds a strong academic foundation.

Faith Formation

Biblical values and Christian character woven into every day of learning.

Safe Community

A nurturing environment where every child is known, valued, and protected.

Whole Child

Developing mind, body, and spirit — preparing children for every season ahead.
